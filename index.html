Course overview

Introduction to Data Science is a hands-on course intended for students with little to no coding experience, in which you learn how to use R to collect, manipulate, analyze, and visualize data.

Schedule

The class time will be divided as follows:

  • 8:35 am to 9:25 am: lecture & Q&A in-person (McCain 1170) and on Teams.

  • 9:35 am to 11:25 am: Lab time with in-person (Rowe 3080) with Teams support.

In-person attendance will remain optional for the entire semester. When in-person teaching resumes, the lectures will be hybrid (in-person, with a recorded Teams broadcast). The labs will be in person, with virtual support available via Teams. Of course, I am always available for support outside of class hours and you can book a meeting with me here.
